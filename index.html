IEEE SATELLITE 2022 - Hybrid Event

IEEE Satellite 2022 will be held as a hybrid event. Presenters may present their papers in person in Shenzhen or virtually. Presenters should choose the presentation format after their papers have been accepted.


Welcome to IEEE SATELLITE 2022!

The 2022 IEEE International Conference on Satellite Computing will be held from 25 to 27 October 2022 in the beautifu and warm city of Shenzhen, China. The conference aims to become a prime international forum for both researchers and industry practitioners to exchange the fundamental advances in state of the art and practice of Satellite computing in the field of Computer Science and Electronic Engineers.
